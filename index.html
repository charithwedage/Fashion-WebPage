The Bespoke Service

Made precisely
for you.

No two bodies are the same. No two lives are the same. Our bespoke service begins with understanding you — your work, your social life, your preferences — and builds from there. Every decision, from cloth weight to button spacing, is yours.

B
How It Works

The five stages
of bespoke.

01

Discovery

An unhurried conversation about your wardrobe needs, lifestyle, and aesthetic preferences.

02

Cloth

Browse our archive of 4,000+ cloths from the world's finest mills. Our cutter will guide you.

03

Measure

Forty-one individual measurements taken by our head cutter. Your pattern is cut by hand.

04

Baste

A basted fitting in the rough garment, before any finishing. Alterations at this stage are unlimited.

05

Collect

Final fitting and collection. Your garment is pressed, packaged, and yours for life.

Our Atelier

London, since
1892.

Maison Ardent was founded in Mayfair in 1892 by George Ardent, a Frenchman who had trained under the finest houses in Paris before bringing his craft to London. For over a century, the house has occupied the same address on Mount Street — a quiet, unhurried corner of the city where time appears to move differently.

Today the house is led by the third generation of the Ardent family. The tools on the cutting table are the same tools George used. The philosophy has not changed.
